Welcome to The Torpedo Lounge

A cigar is a tightly-rolled bundle of dried and fermented tobacco leaf, rolled in a series of types and sizes, that is ignited so that its smoke may be drawn into the mouth. Cigar tobacco is grown in significant quantities in Brazil, Cameroon, Cuba, the Dominican Republic, Honduras, Indonesia, Mexico, Ecuador, Nicaragua, Panama, the Philippines, Puerto Rico, Canary Islands (Spain), Italy and the Eastern United States.

Dont Miss The Big Smoke in Miami

— by Cigar Aficionado magazine

Big Smoke Miami. Held around the United States every year, The Big Smokes offer a unique opportunity for cigar lovers to meet and celebrate the pleasures of smoking fine, handmade cigars alongside fellow aficionados and representatives from the cigar industry. Attendees smoke premium cigars, savor premium spirits and taste great food from local restaurants, and get a chance to meet some of the biggest names in the cigar industry and spend time with the faces behind their favorite brands.

If you're curious about what to expect at a Big Smoke, we've created this feature especially for you. Read on to find about what goes on at a Big Smoke, to check out our Big Smoke schedule, and to peruse our gallery of photos from our most recent Big Smoke. Who knows? You might even spot yourself.
